Wave Interference

Constructive Interference

When two waves meet in phase, their amplitudes add up, creating a wave with greater amplitude.

Destructive Interference

When two waves meet out of phase, their amplitudes cancel each other out.

Standing Waves

When two identical waves travel in opposite directions, they create a standing wave pattern.
